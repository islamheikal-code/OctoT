The Sunken Scriptorium

  • mid-afternoon light. Floor-to-ceiling bookshelves frame a tall
  • arched window, where dust motes drift lazily in the quiet air.

  • Terracotta tiles warmed by an open hearth give this kitchen a
  • welcoming, rustic hum. Copper pots and braided garlic
  • hanks hang from heavy timber ceiling beams, while a
  • scrubbed pine table rests at the center, dusted with flour.

  • surrounds a ornate marble fireplace.

    • The Velvet Parlor

  • Slanted ceilings lined with raw pine boards create an
  • intimate, nook-like sanctuary. Soft string lights crisscross
  • beneath exposed beams, illuminating layers of woven kilim
  • rugs, floor cushions, and trailing pothos vines.

    • The Sunken Hold

  • Constructed from tight, seamless granite blocks,
  • this small chamber is designed to swallow sound entirely.
  • There are no decorative carvings or inscriptions here
  • only a single, unadorned stone sarcophagus resting in the absolute center.

    • The Inquisitor’s Scriptorium

  • Massive crystal chandeliers hang from vaulted ceilings

    • The Solitary Crypt
